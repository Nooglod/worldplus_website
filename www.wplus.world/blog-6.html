Sight Day 10.10

  • Oct 10, 2020

The “Alsiin Gegee 10.10” (“Far Light 10.10”) event has been organized every year since October 10 ( World Vision Day) 2019. There are about 12,000 visually impaired people in Mongolia, of which about 3,500 live in Ulaanbaatar. Since events dedicated to the special needs of these people are not regularly held in our city, we organize a concert catering to their needs and in the process, work to create a small, tight-knit community. Every October 10, acclaimed poets and singers gather to deliver the most excellent and foremost of their works to 03. Social responsibility our guests. The concert is organized with the cooperation of the Mongolian Association of the Visually Impaired and School No. 116 for the Visually Impaired, about 800 people participated in the 2019 “Far Light 10.10” concert, and the 2020 concert was attended by more than 1,000 people. However, in 2021, it was not possible to organize activities due to the COVID-19 restrictions.
